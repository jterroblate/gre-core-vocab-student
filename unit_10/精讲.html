001

retrieve

/rɪˈtriːv/ · v. · Page 133

to get something back or find stored information

找回；取回；检索

联想

retrieve 比 get 更具体：把丢失、存放或记录里的东西重新拿回来。

意思、用法与例句

找回丢失或存放的东西

强调 get back。 object, bag, key, possession。

常见表达retrieve a bagretrieve lost propertyretrieve something from

The diver retrieved the camera from the bottom of the lake.

查看翻译和讲解

潜水员从湖底取回了相机。

retrieve something from 表示从某处取回。

检索信息或数据

用于 computer, memory, archive。

常见表达retrieve dataretrieve a fileretrieve information

The program can retrieve old records in a few seconds.

查看翻译和讲解

这个程序能在几秒钟内检索旧记录。

retrieve data/records 是技术和学术语境常见。

同义 / 反义

近义词

get back
取回
She got back the book she had lent him. 基础表达。
recover
找回；恢复
The police recovered the stolen painting. recover 与 retrieve 很近。
reclaim
收回；重新获得
The community reclaimed the abandoned land. reclaim 常带重新占有。
extract
提取
The software extracts data from scanned documents. extract 用于从材料中取出信息。
retain
保留
The archive retains copies of old maps. retain 是保留，retrieve 是找回或检索。

反义词

lose
丢失
He lost the original file. lose 与 retrieve 相反。
misplace
放错地方
I misplaced my keys again. misplace 是找回前的状态。

词族 / 派生

retrieve 的名词 retrieval 常用于 information retrieval。

这里强调把损坏的物体或建筑重新修复。

常见表达reconstruct a buildingreconstruct a vasereconstruct after war

Archaeologists reconstructed the vase from dozens of fragments.

查看翻译和讲解

考古学家用几十块碎片复原了花瓶。

这里是按碎片修复原貌。

根据证据重构事件或历史

用于 event, scene, history, argument。 event, scene, history, process。

常见表达reconstruct eventsreconstruct the pastreconstruct a scene

Detectives tried to reconstruct the events leading up to the fire.

查看翻译和讲解

侦探试图重构火灾前发生的事件。

抽象义强调从证据复原。

同义 / 反义

近义词

rebuild
重建
The city rebuilt the bridge after the flood. rebuild 偏建筑或系统。
restore
修复
The museum restored the damaged painting. restore 强调恢复原状。
reassemble
重新组装
The mechanic reassembled the engine. reassemble 强调部件重新组合。
recreate
再现；重现
The film recreates the atmosphere of the 1920s. recreate 可指重现效果。
deconstruct
解构；分析拆解
The critic deconstructs the myth of progress. deconstruct 不等于 reconstruct。

反义词

demolish
拆毁
The old factory was demolished. demolish 与重建相反。
destroy
摧毁
The storm destroyed many homes. destroy 是破坏。

词族 / 派生

re- 表示 again；construct 是建造，所以 reconstruct 是重新建造或重构。

查看翻译和讲解

药退了烧，却留下持续的虚弱感。

这里强调残留影响。

同义 / 反义

近义词

lasting
持久的
The accident had lasting effects. lasting 更容易理解。
remaining
剩余的；残留的
The remaining evidence was weak. remaining 更中性。
persistent

residual

反义词

fleeting
短暂的

transient
短暂的；暂时的

falsehood

/ˈfɔːlshʊd/ · n. · Page 133

an untrue statement or the state of being false

谎言；虚假说法

联想

false 是假的；falsehood 可以指一句谎言，也可指虚假性本身。

意思、用法与例句

不真实的陈述或宣传

用于 claim, statement, rumor, propaganda。 claim, statement, rumor, myth。

常见表达spread falsehoodsrepeat a falsehoodexpose a falsehood

The article exposed several falsehoods in the official account.

查看翻译和讲解

文章揭露了官方叙述中的几处虚假说法。

falsehoods 常指可被揭穿的不实陈述。

某说法本身的虚假性

更抽象，用于 truth/falsehood 对照。 argument, assumption, doctrine。

常见表达the falsehood of a claimtruth and falsehooddistinguish falsehood from truth

The experiment demonstrated the falsehood of the old assumption.

查看翻译和讲解

实验证明了旧假设的虚假性。

这里是抽象名词用法。

同义 / 反义

近义词

lie
谎言
The witness told a lie. lie 更日常直接。
untruth
不真实的话
The statement was an untruth. untruth 比 lie 稍弱。
fabrication
捏造
The story was a complete fabrication. fabrication 强调编造出来。
deception
欺骗
The scheme depended on deception. deception 指欺骗行为或状态。
falsity
虚假性
The court examined the falsity of the statement. falsity 更抽象，falsehood 更常指具体假话。

反义词

truth
真相；真实
The truth emerged after the investigation. truth 是直接反义。
fact
事实
The claim was supported by fact. fact 强调客观事实。

词族 / 派生

falsehood = false + -hood，表示“假的状态或假话”。

099

liable

/ˈlaɪəbl/ · adj. · Page 145

likely to do something; legally responsible for something

可能会……的；负有责任的

联想

liable 有两条常见线：be liable to do 表示“容易/可能会”，be liable for 表示“对……负有责任”。

意思、用法与例句

容易发生某种情况或风险

多出现在 be liable to do，表示可能出现某种结果。 risk, tendency, health, behavior, accident。

常见表达be liable to faintbe liable to failbe liable to change

Patients are liable to faint if they stand up too suddenly.

查看翻译和讲解

病人如果站得太突然，可能会晕倒。

be liable to do 表示容易发生某种情况。

The old bridge is liable to collapse under heavy traffic.

查看翻译和讲解

这座旧桥在繁忙交通下可能会倒塌。

liable to collapse 常带风险感。

在法律或责任上需要负责

常用 be liable for，表示对损失、债务或后果承担责任。 law, finance, insurance, contracts。

常见表达be liable for damagesbe held liableliable for the cost

The company may be liable for damages if the product proves unsafe.

查看翻译和讲解

如果产品被证明不安全，公司可能需要承担损害赔偿责任。

liable for damages 是法律语境中的自然表达。

同义 / 反义

近义词

likely
可能的
The plan is likely to change. likely 更中性，liable to 常带不利风险。
responsible
负责的
The driver was responsible for the accident. responsible 对应 liable for 的责任义。
prone
易于……的
The region is prone to floods. prone to 与 liable to 接近，常指倾向或风险。
accountable
应负责的
Officials must be accountable for their decisions. accountable 强调可被问责。
libel
诽谤
The newspaper was sued for libel. libel 是诽谤，liable 是可能或负责。

反义词

unlikely
不太可能的
The error is unlikely to occur again. unlikely 与 liable to 的可能性相反。
exempt
免除责任的；豁免的
Small firms were exempt from the new tax. exempt 与 liable for 的责任义相反。

词族 / 派生

liable 的学习重点在两个介词结构：liable to do 和 liable for something。
