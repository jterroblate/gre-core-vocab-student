diversify

/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪ/ · verb · Page 339

to make or become more varied

使多样化；变得多样

联想

diverse 是“多样的”；diversify 就是让构成不再单一。

意思、用法与例句

让构成从单一变成多样

diversify 强调增加种类或来源，降低单一依赖。

常见表达diversify the economydiversify investmentsdiversify the curriculum

The country tried to diversify its economy beyond oil.

查看翻译和讲解

这个国家试图让经济不再只依赖石油。

diversify the economy 强调减少单一依赖。

Investors diversify their portfolios to reduce risk.

查看翻译和讲解

投资者通过分散投资组合来降低风险。

金融语境中指分散投资。

同义 / 反义

近义词

vary
使变化；变化
The methods vary from school to school. vary 表示变化；diversify 强调增加多样性。
broaden
拓宽
The program broadened its course offerings. broaden 强调范围扩大。
differentiate
区分；使不同
The brand tried to differentiate itself. differentiate 强调区别开来。

反义词

standardize
使标准化
The company standardized its procedures. standardize 与 diversify 的多样化方向相反。
homogenize
使同质化
Mass media can homogenize culture. homogenize 强调变得相似。

联想

obsess

/əbˈses/ · verb · Page 350

to occupy someone’s mind constantly and often troublingly

使着迷；使过分困扰；念念不忘

联想

obsession 是“执念”；obsess 是让某个想法一直占着脑子。

意思、用法与例句

某个想法反复占据脑子

obsess 可作及物动词，也常见 be obsessed with，强调持续、过度关注。

常见表达be obsessed withobsess over detailsan idea obsesses someone

She became obsessed with finding the missing letter.

她开始一心想着找到那封失踪的信。

评论者认为这部小说反复围绕记忆与失去展开。
